// The Problem

Auditing freight invoices is tedious and easy to get wrong.

Every row of every workbook needs a human to read a dozen columns, decide what's off, and type the right reason in the Anmerkung column. Different forwarders, different rules.

SLOW
Hours per file
Hundreds of rows × multiple forwarders × multiple files per week.
INCONSISTENT
Wording drifts
Two auditors write the same finding with different words. Reports get noisy.
EASY TO MISS
Tiny deltas hide
A €0.40 difference is easy to overlook on row 423 of sheet 7.

// Bonus V

Diff Mode — train the engine.

Drop the tool's output (A) and the hand-corrected truth (B). Every disagreement becomes a labeled training case you can filter, export, or send straight into the Rule Tester.

WRONG
Both filled, disagree
Rule fired, but with the wrong wording.
MISSED
A empty, B filled
A rule should have fired but didn't.
OVERFIRED
A filled, B empty
A rule fired when it shouldn't.
DRIFT
Engine moved
Rules changed since A was generated.

Your data stays in your browser.

100% CLIENT-SIDE
